About Me

Kevin beeler was born in Cleveland, Ohio on April 22, 1989, kevin grew up in Brooklyn's drug-infested Marcy Projects. He used rap as an escape, appearing for the first time on Yo! MTV Raps in 1989. After selling millions of records with his Beeler's Record label, Kevin created his own clothing line and founded an entertainment company. He wed popular singer and actress Dennyela Reever in 2015. In addition to his award-winning hip-hop albums, Kevin is known for an array of successful business interests, as well as his marriage to super model Denneyla.

In Kevin's down time he enjoy's practicing his web developing skills, making strong cocktails, painting portriats, and training his poodle not to eat everything in the house.
